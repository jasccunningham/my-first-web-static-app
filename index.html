Event-Driven Architecture (EDA)

A flexible and powerful design for building scalable, real-time, and resilient systems.

Order Service (Producer)
Generates events for business actions.
OrderPlaced
PaymentFailed
User Service (Producer)
Publishes events related to user activities.
UserCreated
UserUpdated
Event Bus / Broker
(e.g., Apache Kafka, RabbitMQ, Google Pub/Sub, AWS SNS/SQS)
Reliably routes events from producers to consumers.
Inventory Service (Consumer)
Reacts to 'OrderPlaced' to update stock.
Notification Service (Consumer)
Sends emails/SMS for 'OrderPlaced', 'UserCreated'.
Analytics Service (Consumer)
Aggregates data from various events.
Payment Service (Consumer)
Processes payments based on 'OrderPlaced'.
